About Us

Our Story, Vision & Values

Our History

Corban Construction Limited is a registered company fully owned and operated in Kenya. The company was incorporated in the year 2007. The directors of the Company are two, namely, Joseph K. Maina and Francis G. Macharia. The core business is building construction, civil works, and Mechanical Installations.

Our Vision

To be the preferred provider of cost effective and innovative construction solutions to our customers. Our vision defines the platform of our business for our clients.

Our Mission

To provide cost-effective and innovative construction solutions that satisfy the needs of our customers whilst guaranteeing continuous development and benefit to our employees.

Our Values

  1. Understanding and satisfying our customer's needs.
  2. Providing equal opportunities for the development of our people.
  3. Promote honesty and integrity amongst the employees.
  4. Promote a healthy and safe working environment.

Our Journey
